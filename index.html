Jumbly Grindrod

Lecturer in Philosophy

University of Reading  ·  PhilPeople

I am a lecturer in philosophy at the University of Reading. My primary research interests lie in philosophy of language and AI - specifically language model technology. I am happy to supervise doctoral students on these topics and would welcome expressions of interest.

Before making a turn towards AI, my research interests were in philosophy of language and epistemology, particularly concerning questions of context-sensitivity.
